Monica Alexander

I am a first-year PhD candidate in the Demography Department at the University of California, Berkeley. I am interested in applications of statistics to social issues such as maternity, migration and economic outcomes.

I work at the ANU's Centre for Aboriginal Economic Policy Research, and as a research assistant. In the past I worked at the Reserve Bank of Australia.

Outside of work and study I am interested in photography (the background photograph is of my hometown, Hobart, Australia) and music.
